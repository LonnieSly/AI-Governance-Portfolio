## 1. Purpose

This policy establishes guidelines for the responsible and secure use of artificial intelligence tools by employees of [Company Name]. It is designed to protect customer data, maintain operational integrity, preserve brand trust, and ensure that AI tools are used in ways consistent with our values and legal obligations.

AI tools offer significant operational benefits — improved response times, reduced staff burden, and enhanced customer experience — but also introduce risks that require clear boundaries and consistent human oversight to manage responsibly. This policy defines those boundaries.

---

## 2. Scope

This policy applies to all full-time employees, part-time staff, contractors, and vendors who access company systems or perform work on behalf of [Company Name] in any operational capacity, across all locations.

---

## 3. Definitions

**Generative AI** refers to artificial intelligence systems capable of producing text, images, or other content in response to user prompts. Examples include ChatGPT, Google Gemini, and similar tools.

**Personally Identifiable Information (PII)** refers to any data that could identify a specific individual, including but not limited to customer names, addresses, order history, payment information, and contact details.

**Approved AI Tools** refers to AI systems explicitly authorized by [Company Name] for business use, as listed in Section 4. Use of any AI tool not on this list for business purposes requires prior written approval from the Policy Owner.

**Human-in-the-Loop (HITL)** refers to the requirement that a qualified employee reviews, approves, and takes accountability for any AI-generated output before it is acted upon, published externally, or communicated to a customer.

**Void Transaction** refers to any POS record indicating a cancelled, recooked, or reversed order entry, which may represent an operational anomaly requiring human context to interpret correctly.

---

## 4. Approved Tools

The following AI tools are approved for business use at [Company Name]:

| Tool | Approved Use | Authorized Users |
|---|---|---|
| [Generative AI Writing Assistant] | Internal communications, marketing content drafts, operational documentation | Management and designated marketing staff |
| [Customer Service LLM Bot] | Customer-facing order inquiries and complaint intake only | Customer-facing staff and bot system |

Use of any AI tool not listed above for business purposes is **prohibited without prior written approval** from the Policy Owner. Approval requests must document the intended use case, data inputs required, and expected outputs.

---

## 5. Acceptable Use

Employees may use approved AI tools to assist with the following activities:

- Drafting internal communications, training materials, and operational documentation
- Generating marketing content for manager review and approval prior to publication
- Summarizing operational reports and performance data for internal distribution
- Responding to routine customer inquiries through the approved customer service bot
- Brainstorming operational process improvements or cost control strategies

All use of approved AI tools must remain within the authorized scope defined in Section 4.

---

## 6. Prohibited Use

Employees may **not** use AI tools — approved or otherwise — for the following purposes:

- **PII Input:** Entering customer PII, payment data, or order history into any AI tool not explicitly authorized to handle such data under a current Data Processing Agreement
- **Unreviewed External Publication:** Publishing, sending, or posting AI-generated content to customers, social media, or external platforms without prior human review and approval
- **Autonomous Personnel Decisions:** Using AI tools to make or finalize decisions on employee scheduling, disciplinary actions, performance evaluations, or compensation without manager review
- **Proprietary Data Sharing:** Sharing confidential business information — including pricing strategies, vendor contracts, supplier terms, or financial data — with external AI platforms
- **Adversarial Prompting:** Attempting to manipulate or bypass AI system guidelines, safety controls, or content restrictions through engineered inputs
- **Misrepresentation:** Using AI tools to generate customer communications that misrepresent company policies, promotions, pricing, or menu availability
- **Void Transaction Automation:** Directing or allowing the customer service bot to autonomously respond to, act on, or resolve any customer inquiry originating from a voided transaction record without manager verification

---

## 7. Data Handling Requirements

**Customer PII** must never be entered into generative AI tools unless the tool has been explicitly approved for PII processing and a current Data Processing Agreement is in place with the vendor.

**Testing and Training:** Employees using AI tools for internal training, brainstorming, or process testing must use anonymized, fictional, or redacted scenarios. Real customer names, order histories, or contact details must not be used in test prompts.

**Order History Access:** Customer order history accessed through the customer service bot is subject to the following retention schedule:

| Data State | Access Window | Authorization Required |
|---|---|---|
| Active | 90 days | Bot system — standard operation |
| Archive | 90 days to 12 months | Manager authorization + audit log entry |
| Deletion | After 12 months | Automatic per retention schedule |

This retention schedule aligns with applicable state privacy regulations including the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA). Employees who receive customer data deletion requests must escalate to the Policy Owner immediately.

---

## 8. Human Oversight Requirements

**External Content:** All AI-generated content intended for external communication — including customer-facing messages, marketing materials, and social media posts — must be reviewed and approved by a designated employee before publication. The reviewing employee is accountable for the accuracy, tone, and appropriateness of the final content.

**Customer Service Bot Escalation:** The customer service bot is authorized to handle routine order inquiries autonomously. The bot **must escalate to a human agent** for any of the following:

- Any complaint involving a refund request or billing dispute
- Any inquiry referencing a food safety concern, allergen issue, or illness
- Any customer expression of distress, frustration, or threatening language
- Any inquiry referencing a voided, recooked, or reversed transaction — these require operational context the bot cannot independently verify
- Any situation where the bot's confidence threshold falls below the defined escalation floor

**Staff Accountability:** Employees who review and approve AI-generated outputs are fully accountable for those outputs. "The AI generated it" is not an acceptable defense for inaccurate, harmful, or non-compliant content.

---

## 9. Violations and Consequences

Violations of this policy will be reviewed by the Policy Owner and may result in:

- Formal written warning and mandatory AI governance retraining
- Suspension of AI tool access privileges
- Disciplinary action up to and including termination, depending on severity and intent
- Mandatory regulatory reporting if a violation results in a customer data breach

Employees who discover a potential policy violation — including suspected misuse by a colleague or vendor — must report it to the Policy Owner immediately. Good-faith reporting is protected; retaliation against reporting employees is prohibited.

---

## 10. Policy Review and Ownership

This policy is owned by the **Operations & Compliance Lead** and will be reviewed:

- **Annually** as a standard governance cycle
- **Following any significant change** to approved AI tools, operational AI deployments, or applicable regulations
- **Immediately** following any AI-related incident, breach, or compliance finding

All employees will be notified of material policy updates. Acknowledgment of this policy is required upon hire and following any material revision.
