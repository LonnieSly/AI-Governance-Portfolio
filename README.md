**Lionel Sylvester** | Operations Intelligence Analyst → AI Governance & Risk Assurance

## About This Portfolio
This repository contains professional AI Governance artifacts developed through applied study of the NIST AI Risk Management Framework (AI RMF). Each project addresses a real-world deployment scenario — an LLM-powered customer service bot for a multi-unit restaurant operation — and demonstrates a complete governance lifecycle from risk identification through policy control and vendor assessment.

These artifacts were not generated from a template. They were built through applied governance reasoning, operational domain expertise, and Human-in-the-Loop review — reflecting how professional AI Governance work is actually done.

## Projects
### Project 1 — AI Risk Register
Eight-risk assessment of an LLM-powered customer service bot deployment. Includes likelihood/impact ratings, operational context, mitigation strategies, and NIST AI RMF function mappings. Three risks were identified directly from operational domain expertise — not from a framework checklist.

### Project 2 — AI Acceptable Use Policy
Organizational policy governing employee use of approved AI tools across a multi-unit restaurant operation. Includes restaurant-specific operational controls, Human-in-the-Loop requirements, data retention boundaries, and void transaction SOPs that generic templates miss entirely.

### Project 3 — Vendor AI Intake Questionnaire
Twenty-two-question procurement assessment framework for evaluating AI vendor risk across six governance domains. Designed to surface accountability gaps, data handling risks, and operational continuity concerns before a contract is signed.
