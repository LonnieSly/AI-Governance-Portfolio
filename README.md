# AI Governance Portfolio

**Lionel Sylvester** | Operations Intelligence Analyst → AI Governance & Risk Assurance

## About This Portfolio
This repository contains AI Governance artifacts developed through applied study of the NIST AI Risk Management Framework (AI RMF). Each project addresses a real deployment scenario — an LLM-powered customer service bot for a multi-unit restaurant operation — and demonstrates a complete governance lifecycle from risk identification through policy control and vendor assessment.

## My Background
I bring 20 years of operational risk management experience combined with hands-on AI and machine learning skills including Python, RAG systems, NLP classification, and predictive modeling. This portfolio represents the bridge between that technical and operational foundation and formal AI Governance practice.

## Framework Applied
*   **NIST AI RMF** — GOVERN, MAP, MEASURE, MANAGE functions
*   **NIST CSF 2.0** — Govern, Identify, Protect functions
*   **NIST SP 800-53** — AC-3 Access Enforcement
*   Regulatory alignment — CCPA, state privacy frameworks

## Projects
### Project 1 — AI Risk Register
Eight-risk assessment of an LLM customer service deployment with likelihood/impact ratings, operational context, and NIST AI RMF mappings.

### Project 2 — AI Acceptable Use Policy
Organizational policy governing employee use of approved AI tools, including restaurant-specific operational controls and HITL requirements.

### Project 3 — Vendor AI Intake Questionnaire
Twenty-question procurement assessment framework for evaluating AI vendor risk across six governance domains.

## Key Skills Demonstrated
AI risk identification and rating methodology, policy drafting for non-technical audiences, vendor governance assessment, NIST AI RMF framework application, Human-in-the-Loop control design, operational risk translation to governance language.
